About Me:

Ricardo Rocheta

29 Years Old

Algarve, Portugal

The Early Years: A Curious Mind in Motion

Since I was in diapers, curiosity was my middle name, just ask my mom! My childhood was a whirlwind of sports, from rugby tackles to trampoline flips, surfing waves to skateboarding tricks. These weren't just hobbies, they were my first life coaches, teaching me about dedication, teamwork, and the art of a graceful fall.

The Leap of Independence

At 18, armed with dreams and a suitcase, I left my nest to find my own sky. This quest led me to beach jobs that weren't just jobs; they were chapters in a novel of self-discovery. Lifeguarding, beach maintenance, and bartending. Each role was a new scene, a new character to play.

London Calling: Shaken, Not Stirred

Then came London, the city of dreams and drizzle. Here, I mixed drinks and experiences, starting as a bartender and stirring my way up to bar supervisor. This wasn't just about cocktails, it was about people, stories, and the magic that happens when both mix.

Homecoming and New Horizons

Life took a U-turn back to Portugal, where I embraced a sales role, dealing with the finer things in life: wines, spirits, cigars, and gourmet foods. This wasn't just sales, it was a masterclass in taste, luxury, and the art of negotiation.

The Modelling Sidestep

Simultaneously, while selling fine wines, I stepped into the world of modeling and production assistant. This unexpected venture led me to appear in commercials and a Netflix series. It was a thrilling detour that showcased my adaptability and willingness to embrace new experiences.

The COVID Curveball

As COVID reshaped the world, it redirected my path to construction sites. Amidst a global pause, I was hands-on, building a house and a pool. This period wasn't just about physical building, it was about developing resilience and adaptability in uncertain times.

Back to Normality:

As things started getting back to normal, I took over managing a bar in Quinta do Lago. But, you know, the night owl life isn't for everyone. I felt like Batman, minus the cape and the cool gadgets. So, back to sales I went, first in telecom, then in a vacation rental tech company.

Tech Awakening

Amidst all this, a spark was lit, a fascination with web development. Surrounded by developer friends and tech talks, I dove headfirst into coding. This wasn't just a hobby, it was a calling. I even built a self-balancing board, a testament to my love for challenges and problem-solving.

Today: A Coder with a Story

Now, here I am, a sales expert by day, a coding enthusiast by night, and a lifelong learner always. My life is a tapestry of experiences, each thread woven with passion, curiosity, and a dash of humor.

What Drives Me?

I'm driven by the quest to understand the "Why?" behind everything. Challenges? Bring them on. I thrive on efficiency and solving puzzles. My world orbits around my family, friends, good food, and fine wine. Onewheel is my zen, F1 and MotoGP my Sunday sermons, and the great outdoors my playground.

So, if you're looking for someone who's as passionate about coding as they are about life, you've found your guy. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
